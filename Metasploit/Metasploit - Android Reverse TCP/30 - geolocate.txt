The geolocate commands allows you to locate the phone by retrieving the current lat-long using geolocation.

meterpreter > geolocate

[*] Current Location:
        Latitude:  11.7508381
        Longitude: 79.749323

To get the address: https://maps.googleapis.com/maps/api/geocode/json?latlng=11.7508381,79.749323&sensor=true
