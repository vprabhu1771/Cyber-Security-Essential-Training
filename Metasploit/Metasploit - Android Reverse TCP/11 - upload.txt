The upload command allows you to upload a file to the remote target. The -r option allows you to do so recursively.

meterpreter > upload /home/kali/Downloads/image.jpg

[*] Uploading  : /home/kali/Downloads/image.jpg -> image.jpg
[*] Uploaded -1.00 B of 266.21 KiB (-0.0%): /home/kali/Downloads/image.jpg -> image.jpg
[*] Completed  : /home/kali/Downloads/image.jpg -> image.jpg
